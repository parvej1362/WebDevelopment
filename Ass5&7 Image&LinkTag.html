Places to visit in Kolhapur

New Palace

The New Palace in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, is a stunning example of Indian architecture. It took seven years to build, from 1877 to 1884, and cost about seven lakh rupees. Constructed with black polished stone, this architectural marvel is a favorite among tourists.

Panhala Fort

Panhala fort (also known as Panhalgad and Panhalla (literally "the home of serpents")), is located in Panhala, 20 kilometres northwest of Kolhapur in Maharashtra, India.
